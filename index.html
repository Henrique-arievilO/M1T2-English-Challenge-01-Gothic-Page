What's BabyBat.com?

The BabyBat.com project aims to bring more information to those who are entering the Gothic subculture and don't know where to start. Come with us and you will have many tips for movies, music, books and other content to delve into our community.

The Gothic subculture (also known as "dark" in Brazil) is an urban subculture that began in the UK in the late 1970s and early 1980s. The Gothic subculture is strongly associated with minimalist, expressionist, romantic music genres. or gloomy. Some adepts of the subculture can diversify, appreciating other styles such as dream pop, industrial rock, industrial metal, gothic metal among others.

Authors of Gothic Literature

But not only music survives the Gothic subculture. We also have several exponents in the literature, such as:

  • ⚜ Edgar Allan Poe with the famous poem "The Raven";
  • ⚜ H. P. Lovecraft with "The Call of Cthulhu";
  • ⚜ Bram Stoker and his acclaimed "Dracula";
  • ⚜ Mary Shelley with the classic "Frankenstein";
  • ⚜ Lord Byron and his obscure poem "Manfred"
